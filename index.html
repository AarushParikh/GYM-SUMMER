CREATINE

5g every single day — training and rest days. Mix into post-workout shake. As a vegetarian your baseline creatine is naturally lower, meaning you'll likely respond better than most.

Minimum a gallon of water daily. More on training days. Non-negotiable.

Saturating your muscle cells slowly. No noticeable effect yet — completely normal. Keep going.
Weeks 3–4
Starting to feel stronger. Muscles looking fuller. You'll gain 2–4 lbs — that's water in your muscle cells. Not fat. That's the creatine working.

Fully saturated. Extra reps on every set. Faster recovery between sets. Compounds across hundreds of sessions all summer.

// 07 — Non-Negotiables

THE RULES

01
Sleep 8–9 Hours

Muscle is built while you sleep. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep at 16. You are growing more efficiently right now than you ever will again. Don't waste it staying up late.

02
Form Before Weight

First 2–3 weeks, go lighter and feel the right muscles. A strict 25 lb curl is worth more than a sloppy 40. Bad form causes injuries that end your whole summer in one session.

03
Progressive Overload

Every session, try to do more than last time. One more rep, 5 more lbs. Update your numbers in the tracker. This single principle drives 90% of all muscle growth.

04
Pain vs Soreness

Soreness the day after = normal. Sharp pain during a lift, joint pain, pain that won't go away = stop immediately. One injury can kill your entire summer. Know the difference.

05
Don't Skip Legs

Legs are your biggest muscle group. Training them releases the most testosterone and growth hormone in your body. Skipping legs literally slows down your chest and arm gains.

06
Consistency Wins

You will have bad days and bad weeks. That's normal. What matters is what you do next. One bad week won't ruin your summer. Quitting after a bad week will.
