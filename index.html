Contact: [support email address]. ## Information we collect **Account information.** When you create an account we collect your email address and password. Passwords are handled entirely by our authentication provider, Supabase, using industry-standard hashing — we never see or store your password in plain text. **Profile information.** First and last name, gender, city, state, zip code, dietary restrictions, and your stated interests (hobbies, music, movies/TV, sports/fitness, cuisines). You choose what to share; most fields are optional. **Location.** With your permission, we use your device's location (or a city/zip you provide) to find hangout spots near you and to show your position and recommended venues on the in-app map. You can decline location access and enter a zip code manually instead. **Favorite places.** Place names you enter are looked up against a mapping service to get an address and coordinates, which we store so we can show them on your map. **Group session data.** When you host or join a group session, we store the session's room code, location, interest tags, participant names, and swipe (like/skip) choices for the duration of the session, so the group's decision can be calculated. **Usage/recommendation history.** We keep a log of activities suggested to you so we can show your past recommendations. ## How we use your information - To create and secure your account - To generate personalized hangout/venue recommendations - To run group decision-making sessions (room codes, swiping, results) - To show your location and recommended places on a map - To improve the App We do not sell your personal information. ## Third parties we share data with We use the following service providers to operate the App. Each only receives the minimum data needed to perform its function: - **Supabase** — authentication, database, and file storage. Hosts all account and profile data. - **Groq** — generates activity/venue recommendations from your stated interests, mode (solo/date/family/friends), and a general location string (e.g., "Palatine, IL"). Groq does not receive your name, email, or account identifiers. - **TomTom** — looks up addresses/coordinates for places you name. Receives only the place name text you enter. - **Zippopotam.us** — converts a U.S. zip code to a city/state name. Receives only the zip code. - **Apple/Google** — app distribution and, on Android, mapping (Google Maps) for the in-app map. ## Data retention and deletion You can delete your account at any time from **Account → Delete Account** inside the App. This permanently removes your profile, saved places, recommendation history, and session participation records, and deletes your login credentials. This action cannot be undone. ## Children's privacy HangoutHunt is not directed to children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect information from children under that age. ## Your choices - You can decline location permission; the App will ask for a zip code instead. - You can edit or remove your profile information at any time from the Account tab. - You can delete your account and all associated data at any time (see above). ## Changes to this policy We may update this policy from time to time.
